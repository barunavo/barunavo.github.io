About Me

In the vast tapestry of life, I am a Bengali hailing from the enchanting land of Alipurduar, nestled in the captivating state of West Bengal, India. An ardent explorer of flavors, I find solace and delight in the art of cooking, perpetually tantalizing my taste buds with culinary creations.

However, destiny has led me on a captivating journey to the vibrant city of Berlin, Germany. While I navigate the winding streets of this European metropolis, my heart remains intertwined with the vivid hues of my Indian heritage. Accompanying me on this adventure is my beloved wife, Sumana Saha, whose presence adds warmth and joy to each chapter of our lives

Beyond the kitchen and cultural explorations, I am a seasoned Software Engineer by profession. Equipped with a postgraduate engineering degree and an additional feather in my cap—a MBA—my multidimensional expertise empowers me to embrace both the realms of technology and business.

When the pages of work-life turn, I find solace in the world of words. The intriguing realm of detective stories beckons me, whispering tales of suspense, unraveling mysteries one page at a time. With each riveting story, my imagination soars, transcending the boundaries of reality.

Thus, my story is an amalgamation of diverse flavors, cultures, and intellectual pursuits. I strive to blend the spices of life, savoring each moment as I embark on this remarkable journey through the intricate tapestry of existence.
